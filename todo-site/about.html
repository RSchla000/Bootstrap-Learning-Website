About the Company

Meet the ones who keep Midgar and the world ticking along!

President Shinra

He is the president of the Shinra Electric Power Company. He is a hardworking businessman and a cunning politician who rules Midgar with pride, supporting the media with his good favor, and is always rewarding those in his good graces. President Shinra seeks to locate the "promised land" and use its abundant mako energy to build a Neo Midgar. He is the father of Rufus Shinra, the vice president and heir to the company.

Vice President Rufus Shinra

Rufus is the son of President Shinra and the vice president of the Shinra Electric Power Company. He is immensely prideful, cold, and ambitious with a rebellious streak. Rufus aims to break outdated practices and lead the company into a new era. He works closely with the Turks, with Tseng as his right-hand man.
