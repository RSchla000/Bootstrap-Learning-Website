About this Mini-Site

Short notes for teachers and kids explaining the Bootstrap classes used on the site

What the classes do
  • .text-* sets text color (primary, success, danger,...)
  • .card makes a box with padding
  • .btn-group keeps buttons together
  • .nav .nav-item .nav-link make simple menus
