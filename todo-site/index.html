Welcome, to the World of Mako Energy!

Mako is the leading source of energy in todays modern world and Shin-Ra is at the forefront of this revolution.

Company Overview

Initially founded as Shinra Manufacturing Works, the company specialized in weapon manufacture. We then discovered a process to refine the planet's life energy, known as the lifestream to some, into a form of energy we dubbed "mako". Capitalizing on this revolutionary new energy source,we installed mako reactors all over the globe, allowing our influence and financial gains to grow by leaps and bounds.Using Midgar as our base of operations, this massive conglomerate maintains an ironclad grip on the world. Currently, the company focuses on mako energy, arms development, urban planning, space and aeronautics, and food and pharmaceutical production
